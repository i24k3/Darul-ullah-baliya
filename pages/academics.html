Our Educational Programs

Darul Uloom Bilaliya offers structured Islamic education programs designed to nurture scholars who are well-grounded in traditional Islamic sciences while being aware of contemporary challenges.

Hifz-ul-Quran Program

Our comprehensive Quran memorization program enables students to memorize the entire Holy Quran with proper Tajweed and understanding. The program includes:

  • Complete memorization of 30 Juz of Quran
  • Tajweed and proper pronunciation
  • Revision schedules to ensure retention
  • Understanding of memorized portions
  • Preparation for Qirat competitions
Duration: 2-3 Years
Age Group: 8-16 Years
Admissions: Open annually

Aalim Course (Dars-e-Nizami)

The comprehensive 6-year Aalim course follows the renowned Dars-e-Nizami curriculum, producing scholars well-versed in Islamic sciences.

Quranic Sciences

  • Tajweed
  • Tafsir
  • Qirat

Hadith Sciences

  • Sahih al-Bukhari
  • Sahih Muslim
  • Sunan Abu Dawud

Fiqh & Usul

  • Hanafi Fiqh
  • Usul al-Fiqh
  • Comparative Fiqh

Language Studies

  • Arabic Literature
  • Urdu & Persian
  • Grammar & Rhetoric
Duration: 6 Years
Certification: Aalimiyat Degree
Eligibility: Completion of Hifz or equivalent
