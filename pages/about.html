Our Legacy, Mission, and Vision for Islamic Education

Our History & Legacy

Established on 11th Shawwal ul Mukaram 1411 Hijri (1991 CE), Darul Uloom Bilaliya began as a humble institution on a small plot of land (40√ó60 sq. ft.) in Bilalabad, Lal Bazar. Founded with the vision of preserving authentic Islamic knowledge, the institution grew under the spiritual guidance of renowned scholars including Sheikh Maulana Maseehullah Khan (RA).

Through community support and divine blessings, the Darul Uloom has expanded to become one of the leading centers of Deobandi education in Kashmir, currently accommodating over 550 residential students and 60 dedicated staff members.
