Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about supporting our institution

How can I be sure my donation is used properly?

We maintain complete transparency with quarterly financial reports available to all donors. Our accounts are audited annually by an independent firm, and we ensure that 92% of all donations go directly to our programs.

Can I specify how my donation should be used?

Yes, you can designate your donation to specific programs such as student sponsorship, infrastructure development, or library resources. If you don't specify, your donation will be used where it's most needed.

Will I receive updates about the student I sponsor?

Yes, student sponsors receive bi-annual progress reports with academic updates and personal messages from their sponsored students. You'll also receive photos and information about their activities.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Darul Uloom Bilaliya is registered as a charitable institution, and all donations are tax-deductible under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. You will receive a receipt for tax purposes.

Can I visit the institution and meet the students?

We welcome all donors to visit our campus. You can schedule a visit through our contact form, and we would be honored to give you a tour and introduce you to our students and faculty.

How can I become a monthly donor?

You can set up a recurring donation through our online portal. Simply select the "Monthly" option when making your donation, and your payment method will be charged automatically each month.
